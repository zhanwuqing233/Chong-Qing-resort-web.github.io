Hong Ya Dong 洪崖洞

Description

Hongya Cave, formerly known as Hongya Gate, is one of the ancient city gates of Chongqing, located at No. 88 Binjiang Road, Jialing River, Yuzhong District, Chongqing. In 2003, a total investment of 385 million yuan was invested in the demolition and reconstruction of the old city of Hongya Cave area, which was completed and opened in September 2006. Hongya Cave is a key landscape project in Chongqing, with a construction area of 46,000 square meters. The main scenic spots are composed of stilted buildings and antique commercial streets. Hongya Cave has a total of 11 floors, and the lights are turned on at night from 6 p.m. to 10 p.m. Expected to hang group floor view Hongya dripping Cui, visit the city of the old street to admire the Bayu culture, hot city of the hot pot to see the two rivers, the world food. It has formed the business form of "one state, three wonders, four streets and eight scenes", which reflects the Bayu culture and leisure business form. In November 2007, Chongqing Hongyadong folk style area was rated as a national AAAA tourist attraction. On November 18, 2020, Hongya Cave was included in the "Top Ten New Landmarks of Chengdu-Chongqing Cultural Tourism". In December 2021, Hongya Cave was included in the "second batch of Chongqing Historical Place Name Protection List".
