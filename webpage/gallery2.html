Chongqing Song Meiling old residence exhibition Hall

重庆宋美龄旧居陈列馆

Description

The exhibition hall of Soong Ching Ling's Old Residence is the residence of Soong Ching Ling during the Anti-Japanese War and the old site of the Central Committee of the "Grand League for the Defense of China". It is a national 2A level tourist area. It is a Chongqing level cultural relic protection unit, but also Chongqing patriotic education base, the national united front traditional education base.
