Yangtze River Bridge 长江大桥

Description

The Chongqing Yangtze River Bridge, also known as the "Shipo Yangtze River Bridge", is a river crossing connecting Yuzhong District and Nanan District in Chongqing, China. It is located over the Yangtze River channel and is the first super-large urban highway bridge in the middle reaches of the Yangtze River. It is also an important part of the city's main road in the southwest of Chongqing. Chongqing Yangtze River Bridge started construction on November 26, 1977; Opened to traffic on July 1, 1980; Construction of the double-track bridge started on December 28, 2003; It opened to traffic on September 26, 2006. Chongqing Yangtze River Bridge starts from Shibanpo Interchange in the north, crosses the Yangtze River Waterway in the upper, and reaches Huanggedu Interchange in the south; The total length of the old bridge is 1120 meters, and the total length of the double bridge is 1103.5 meters. The road is a two-way eight-lane urban main road with a design speed of 60 km/h.
