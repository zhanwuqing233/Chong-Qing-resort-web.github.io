Description

Liziba Park, also known as Liziba Anti-Japanese War Site Park, is located next to Liziba Monorail Station in Yuzhong District of Chongqing, backed by Eiling and north of Jialing River, with a total length of 1.8 kilometers and an area of 120,000 square meters. It is the first Anti-Japanese War site park in Chongqing. In 1938, Chongqing gained worldwide fame as a wartime secondary capital. Today, walking in the traditional style area of Liziba, you can also touch the many remaining relics of the Anti-Japanese War, which are the witnesses of the political, economic, cultural, military, diplomatic, financial and other aspects of the Anti-Japanese War period, condensed into the historical and cultural light and shadow of the wartime capital.
